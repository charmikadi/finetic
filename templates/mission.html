About Us

We are a group of ambitious Canadian students passionate about helping patients living with chronic conditions. Our team was motivated to create the platform Finetic after learning that 44% of Canadian adults 20+ have at least 1 of 10 common chronic conditions. We aim to create a positive change by implementing Finetic in the real world.
